Panda

The panda, also known as the giant panda, is a bear native to south-central China. It is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.

Dolphin

The dolphin is a highly intelligent marine mammal. Known for their playful nature and remarkable agility, dolphins are beloved creatures found in oceans and seas around the world.

Arctic Fox

The arctic fox is a small fox species native to the Arctic regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Its thick fur and rounded body help it adapt to the cold climate, making it a remarkable Arctic predator.
